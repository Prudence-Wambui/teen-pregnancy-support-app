Intro: Love Meets Revenge

Theres just something intoxicating about revenge romance stories. Maybe its the high stakes of love mixed with betrayal. Maybe its the undeniable tension between characters who want to destroy each other as much as they desire one another. Or maybe its the deliciously messy realization that revenge and love are two sides of the same coin.

If youre ready for fiery passion, jaw-dropping twists, and romances born out of chaos, betrayal, and redemption, this list is for you. Here are 20 revenge romance novels you wont be able to put down. Plus, dont miss the merch surprise at the endbecause nothing says team chaos like some bookish swag!

The Ultimate Revenge Romance Reading List

1. Vicious by L.J. Shen

Vicious is as cold as his name suggests, but his obsession with Emilia burns hot. This enemies-to-lovers revenge romance is steamy, angsty, and addictive.

2. Sweet Ruin by Kresley Cole

A steamy paranormal romance where revenge drives the action but forbidden love takes center stage. Coles addictive writing will keep you hooked.

3. Toxic by Rachel Van Dyken

This deeply emotional romance follows two broken souls tied by secrets, betrayal, and a second chance they never expected.

4. Cruel Intentions by Siobhan Davis

Filled with dark secrets, manipulation, and forbidden passion, this high school revenge romance is as ruthless as its title implies.

5. Ruthless People by J.J. McAvoy

A mafia queen marries a rival in this gripping enemies-to-lovers romance. Expect fiery chemistry, intense power plays, and plenty of revenge.

8. Beautiful Disaster by Jamie McGuire

While not a traditional revenge romance, this chaotic love story between bad boy Travis and good girl Abby is fueled by tension and secrets.

9. Indebted Series by Pepper Winters

This dark, twisted romance about debts, power, and revenge will leave you breathless. Its not for the faint of heart, but its unforgettable.

10. Paper Princess by Erin Watt

Thrown into a world of wealth, power, and betrayal, Ella navigates revenge and forbidden attraction in this addictively dramatic romance.

11. Twisted Pride by Cora Reilly

This mafia romance is brimming with revenge, tension, and an undeniable pull between two people from rival families.

12. The Darkest Temptation by Danielle Lori

Revenge and forbidden love collide in this dark mafia romance, where loyalty and desire are constantly at odds.

13. Sparrow by L.J. Shen

An arranged marriage, a grudge, and a tangled web of lies create the perfect storm for this unforgettable revenge romance.

14. The Kiss Thief by L.J. Shen

Francescas first kiss is stolen by a ruthless politician with revenge on his mind. This book is a deliciously twisted enemies-to-lovers masterpiece.

15. Twisted Loyalties by Cora Reilly

This dark romance blends loyalty, betrayal, and sizzling chemistry as a mafia enforcer faces the ultimate temptation.

16. The Maddest Obsession by Danielle Lori

A reckless heroine and a cold, calculating hero cross paths in this intoxicating mafia romance. Revenge has never been so complicatedor so hot.

17. Hidden Seams by Alessandra Torre

A fashion moguls secret past comes back to haunt him when a woman shows up with questionsand revenge on her mind.

18. Retribution by Natasha Knight

This dark mafia romance follows a man on a mission for vengeance and a woman caught in the crossfire of his plans.

19. Always Crew by Tijan

A story of loyalty, betrayal, and redemption, this gripping romance follows a crew thatll do anything to protect each othereven if it means taking revenge.

20. Raw by Belle Aurora

This dark and gritty romance features a twisted antihero who cant decide if he wants to destroy the heroine or save her. Its raw, emotional, and unforgettable.
