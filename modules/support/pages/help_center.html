Help Center & Support

Common Questions

  • How do I update my due date? - Go to Pregnancy > Due Date Entry and submit a new date.
  • How do I schedule a prenatal visit? - Go to Visits > Schedule Visit.
  • What should I eat during pregnancy? - See Nutrition Tips under the Nutrition section.
  • How do I join a support group? - Contact support below to get connected.

Emotional and Mental Health Support

Pregnancy can be overwhelming. Remember, you are not alone. Here are resources available to you:

  • Talk to a counselor at your healthcare facility.
  • Join a local teen mother support group.
  • Use mindfulness apps like Calm or Headspace for relaxation.
  • Speak to trusted family members or friends.
