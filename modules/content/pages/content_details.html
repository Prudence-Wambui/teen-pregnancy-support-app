Pregnancy Nutrition Tips for Teen Mothers

By TeenParent+ Health Team • Updated: May 5, 2025

Nutrition plays a crucial role during pregnancy, especially for teen mothers. Your body is supporting both you and your baby’s growth, so choosing the right foods is essential.

Why Nutrition Matters

Teens are still growing themselves, which means your body needs even more nutrients like iron, calcium, folic acid, and protein to ensure healthy development for both you and the baby.

What to Eat

  • Fruits and Vegetables: Packed with vitamins, fiber, and minerals.
  • Whole Grains: Provide long-lasting energy and key nutrients.
  • Lean Proteins: Such as beans, eggs, chicken, and fish.
  • Dairy: For calcium, try yogurt, cheese, or milk.

What to Avoid

Limit caffeine, sugary snacks, and processed foods. Avoid alcohol and smoking completely.

“Eating well helps both you and your baby grow strong. Small changes make a big difference!”
